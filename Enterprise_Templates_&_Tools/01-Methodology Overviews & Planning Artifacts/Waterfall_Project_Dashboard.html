Defect Management Process (P1/P2 Standard)

  1. Defects are logged in the designated tracking tool (e.g., Jira).
  2. Triage & Priority: Defects are immediately prioritized (P1-Critical, P2-High, P3-Medium, P4-Low).
  3. **Fixing:** P1 and P2 defects **must** be fixed and re-tested within 48 hours.
  4. Closure: Only the QA Lead can close a defect after successful re-testing.
